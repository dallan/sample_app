Contributions

 

'Last Contribution Amount' is the last payment in your local currency applied to Computershare's systems.

'Last Requested Contribution Amount' is the last contribution change request you submitted. Note: if you have suspended your contribution this will still show your last requested contribution amount and NOT zero. This will enable you to restart or change the contribution in the next Alteration Period.

'Balance' is the current amount available in plan currency to purchase Contribution Shares.

If you wish to change your contribution amount, during permitted periods, please click on the 'Change' link, when available, and enter a new contribution amount which must be within the monthly minimum and maximum limits.

If you wish to suspend your contributions to the plan please click on the 'Suspend' link and re-enter your PIN. To recommence contributing, during permitted periods, at the 'last contribution amount', click on the 'Restart' link, when available, which will appear if you have suspended your contribution.

USAPAY and CANPAY participants

Please note that you are not able to use this to change or suspend your contributions, please refer to your Stock Purchase Plan enrolment tool on your internal website to complete this.
